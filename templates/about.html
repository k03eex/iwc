About

This website is final project of MSc Internet and Wireless Computing students at University of York. The project demonstrates principle of operations of Internet of Things, which are significantly developing technology. It focuses on three different directions as Embedded Systems (sensors, microcontrollers), Communications and Web tecnologies (Cloud Computing, Big Data and Web applications). Consequntly, each student in the group is responsible for each field.

The system can help students, teachers and researchers from Environment and Biology departments to investigate biodiversity or any other hypothesis based on the data. A supervisor as team leader helped students to solve issues and guided to be on the right way during the development of the project.
